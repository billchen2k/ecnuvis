We are dedicated to the research of visualization algorithms and interaction for large-scale and high-dimensional data, the design and implementation of visual analytics systems, as well as novel applications of deep learning methods and large language models in visualization. Our recent research interests include:

- Spatio-temporal data visual analysis and urban computing
- Large language models and Fintech
- AIGC and visual storytelling
- Visualization design and invertible visualization
